About Us

The Bangladesh Journalists Rights Forum - BJRF was established in 2002 with journalist Habibur Rahman as Chairman and Journalist Ataur Rahman as General Secretary as a Media Network organization with the support and active participation of Journalists and Professionals Organizations in Bangladesh.
USS has built its success and reputation by offering a consistently professional and high quality services to his customers achieved through a team of experienced seafarers and their highly proficient shore based counterparts.USS is fully geared to meet the growing needs of its principle with the backing of an efficient marketing and support team.
